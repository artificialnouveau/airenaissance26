Ranked by readiness to join a Large Model Training Consortium

A Large Model Training Consortium means many states or firms pooling compute, data, and money to build frontier AI together, instead of depending on the US or China. Each group is ranked on its own scale: left to right runs blue to red, from the most likely to commit to such a consortium to the least, as the end labels describe. Tap any name to open its card.
